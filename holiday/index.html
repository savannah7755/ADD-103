Easter Egg Hunt

Information on this years egg hunt!

The biggest Easter egg hunt of 2024 is set to take place on April 20th, from 10 AM to 4 PM, at Central Park's expansive open fields. This family-friendly event is designed to accommodate all ages, featuring separate hunts tailored to different age groups to ensure everyone can join in the fun. Young children will have their own designated area filled with brightly colored eggs and smaller treats, while older kids and teens will have a more challenging hunt with larger prizes. Adults can enjoy a relaxing break with a variety of snacks, including freshly baked cookies, sandwiches, and beverages, available throughout the day. In addition to the egg hunts, there will be face painting, live entertainment, and craft stations for all to enjoy, making it a festive day for the entire community. After the excitement of the Easter egg hunt, get ready for an unforgettable musical experience as a renowned live band takes the stage. Their electrifying performance promises to infuse the day with vibrant energy and infectious rhythms, creating a perfect finale to the festivities. As the band plays a mix of their greatest hits and crowd-pleasing tunes, the atmosphere will be charged with joy and celebration, inviting everyone to dance and sing along. This dynamic live set will be the highlight of the day, ensuring that the Easter celebration ends on a high note with music that resonates long after the last note fades.

Following the joyful Easter egg hunt, everyone is warmly invited to join us for a spirited church service that celebrates the season’s hope and renewal. The service will be filled with uplifting hymns and a meaningful message that reflects the essence of Easter. Afterward, families can capture the fun of the day with photos alongside our special guest, the Easter Bunny, who will be available for pictures. For those children who couldn’t participate in the egg hunt, don’t worry—goodie bags brimming with Easter treats will be handed out to ensure everyone leaves with a smile. It's a wonderful opportunity to come together in faith and fellowship, creating lasting memories with loved ones.
